About Me

Hey! My name is Alois Vidlák, I am 17 and I love photography. Most of the time I shoot cars, but sometimes I like to try other things too. I am always amazed at how creative people can be with a camera, and I enjoy telling a little story with every photo I take. Photography got me hooked when a friend of mine bought a camera. I borrowed it at first, loved it so much that I got my own, and have been exploring photography and motorsports ever since.
